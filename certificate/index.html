Test and Product Engineering Certificate Program

Certificates show potential employers that the student has taken a concentration of electives making the student potentially more marketable. RHIT has developed a certificate program to provide students with a specialization in test and product engineering.

The certificate requires:

  • Two test and product engineering courses
  • Three additional courses to broaden student background
      • Elective courses from the following domains:
    • Integrated circuit design
    • Printed circuit board design
    • Microelectronic system design
    • Integrated circuit fabrication
    • Statistical validation of data
