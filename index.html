Courses teaching Test and Product Engineering

TPE stands for Test and Product Engineering. The Test and Product Engineering courses developed at the Rose-Hulman Institute of Technology have the following characteristics:

Use of automated test equipment to validate the specifications for integrated circuit chips including:

  • Analog integrated circuits
  • Mixed-Signal integrated circuits (DAC and ADC)
  • Microcontrollers

Course and lab opportunities designed to develop critical thinking skills.
Emphasis on test-time vs. test-accuracy tradeoff.
Hands-on labs provide student use of industry-grade test equipment and experiences with industry test procedures.
Course and labs developed in conjunction with industrial partners:



Analog TPE


Digital TPE


Mixed TPE
