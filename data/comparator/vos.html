Comparator Offset Voltage (Vos) and Hysteresis (Vhyst) Data

The offset and hysteresis test was performed by holding Vinm constant and sweeping Vinp in 0.5mV increments to find the lower trip point and the upper trip point. The offset voltage (Vos) and the hysteresis voltage (Vhyst) were calculated from the trip points.

This data set shows the repeatability of the results (test1 to test5), the process variation between 5 chips (chip1 to chip5) and the reproducibility of the test by looking at two different sites (site1 and site2).
